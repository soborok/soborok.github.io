Thinking Styles

October 25, 2014

Concrete or Abstract? Sequential or Random

Taking a short thinking style test was interesting when I was preparing for DBC. Thinking Styles is Anthony Gregorc's work and it has 4 qualities which a person use more when they think and learn.
My thinking style is AS and CS. I'd like to have just one style but my test score was the same on AS and CS. I have very low score in CR and AR which has Random ability. I am convinced by Anthony Gregorc's work cause I am not a person who can randomly do many things at a time and get a good result. I have thought this style of me not allow me to have creativity and flexibility. However, I am very well orgarnized and quick leaner once I know the sequence.

DBC and CS + AS

DBC is short-term intense programming course. There is a lot of paring/gourp projects and I need to do a lot of self-study. And I will be learning new programming language and new stuffs. In the first and second week of phase 0 I struggled with study order and resources. I started it late so I was busy catching up early assignments. I didn't get how curriculum is structured, what can be done first and how they are related. This difficulty I had is related to my thinking style.
When I am given some amount of info I take time, think them through, put them in an order and relate them. I need time to absorb and process inputs in me. I will be doing great once I get the flow and structure in my head. I am worried about phase 1 if I wouldn't have time for myself to orgarnize new learning and my thoughts. It would help me if I study beforehand and spend personal time to analyze/orgarnize learning.
Good thing is there will be experts in DBC campus. They give clear feedback and let me reach my goal.

Thinking Styles and Mindset

Before phase 0 started I read about growth vs. fixed mindset. This gave me a good view of my mindset. I agree on many points and I found I got used to fixed mindset. Good news is I can exercise and change my mindset. I focus on process rather than just result and opened my mind to cricticism. I won't get discouraged when I don't accomplish my challenge because my thinking style need consuming time. I will try challenge again, make it mine and take the process as a part of my output.
