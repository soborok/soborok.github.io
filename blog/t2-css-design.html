What is the difference between Margin, Border, and Padding?

October 19, 2014

Eggs in crate

Margin is the space between the element and other element.
Border is the surrounding line of the element.
Padding is the space between the element content and its border.

Imagine you are a yolk surrounded by egg white and covered with egg shell.
Egg shell is the border between you and outside world.
Egg white surrounds yolk with white soft padding.
And the yolk is the content which is most nutritious and you want to show off the most.
Uhoh wait. There are more eggs. They are on your right and left, front and back. They have some distance with you. That is margin.
